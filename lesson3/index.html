Site Purpose

The purpose of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce site is to provide relevant information about what's going on in the city of Rexburg. It will be used to present local news such as important changes, events, new businesses coming to the area, and much more. It will also allow the community to contact local authorities and answer any frequently asked questions.
